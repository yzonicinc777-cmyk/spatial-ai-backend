Core Capabilities

Built different.
Works smarter.

Spatial AI Explorer runs entirely on your device — no cloud, no servers, no compromises. Our WASM-powered engine delivers desktop-grade intelligence through your phone's camera.

On-Device AI Engine
WebAssembly-compiled computer vision runs natively in your browser. Zero round-trips, zero data leaves your device, full speed ahead.
Template Matching
Capture any object as a template. Multi-scale Gaussian pyramid search with HOG features and RANSAC verification finds it anywhere in frame.
Spatial Mapping
GPS, compass, and gyroscope fuse together to understand your location and orientation in three-dimensional space — continuously and silently.
Voice Intelligence
Natural language commands let you describe what to find. Whisper your query — the AI listens, understands, and acts immediately.
Real-Time Overlay
Detection results paint directly over your camera view at 60fps. Bounding boxes, confidence scores, and spatial measurements rendered live.
Private by Design
Every pixel stays on your device. No analytics, no profiling, no telemetry. Your camera data is yours — forever. Encrypted local storage only.

Future Goals

Spatial intelligence for every human endeavour.

01

Medicine & Diagnostics

AR overlays that guide surgeons, assist nurses in identifying medications, and help patients understand their own anatomy — real-time spatial intelligence in the operating theatre and clinic.

02

Engineering & Construction

Overlay structural blueprints onto physical sites, identify components in complex machinery, and verify tolerances without instruments — AR as the engineer's second pair of eyes.

03

Education & Learning

Turn any textbook into an interactive 3D experience. Point at a diagram, watch it come alive. Spatial AI will make abstract concepts tangible for the next generation of learners.

04

Give sight to the visually impaired through rich audio descriptions of the world in front of them. Navigate public spaces, read text, identify faces of loved ones — all in real time.

Environmental Science

Identify species in the wild, measure deforestation in real time, monitor pollution — spatial AI as an instrument of planetary stewardship and climate accountability.

Vision for Humanity

I believe technology should expand human capability without compromising human dignity. Spatial AI Explorer is proof that powerful intelligence and radical privacy are not opposites — they are complements. My goal is a world where every person, regardless of technical literacy, can access the full power of AI to understand their environment, make better decisions, and live richer lives.

Privacy as a Human Right

Every camera access, every sensor reading, every template you save stays on your device. I designed Yzonic's architecture from first principles around this axiom: your data is never for sale, never for training models, never transmitted without explicit consent. Privacy is not a compliance checkbox — it is the foundation of trust, and trust is the foundation of everything we build.

Security Commitment

Our WASM sandbox, strict Content Security Policy, and zero external dependency model mean Spatial AI Explorer has an attack surface measured in kilobytes, not megabytes. Regular third-party audits, open-source code, and transparent changelogs are not aspirational goals — they are our minimum standard.
